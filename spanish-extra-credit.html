Things you need to know:

Terremoto =/= tormenta

big tormenta = hurrican

paramedico and hero are valiente

volcan causes lots of destruction

bomberos rescatar/salvar gatos y perros

periodicos contain articulos

when there is a tormenta grande, people are astutado (scared)

salvar la vida- save a life

      -if all lives are saved, no one murrior (dies)

paramedicos estaban consado- the paramedics are tired

ir de prisa- went quickly

 

Short answers?:

In a fire, heroes feel valiant. They rescue the injured and scared.

People who don't escape from a disaster  - se murriren

police and firefighters investigate fires

First person experience of a past storm, like this:

Estaba jugando videojuegos cuando ocurrió la inundación
