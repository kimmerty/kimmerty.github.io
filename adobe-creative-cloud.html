This is a guide on how to get the Adobe Creative Cloud for free. This includes access to good stuff like Photoshop without having to pay that $20 a month. Lets begin.

 

To begin, download this. If you are on Chrome, it will block you from downloading because it is a "malicious" file, but hit Ctrl+J to go to your downloads to recover it. This file ISN'T a virus, even if every single malware scanner will think it is.

Now, go to this website:

 

http://www.adobe.com/creativecloud/start-with-free-creativecloud.html

 

Start a free trial, and then make a Adobe ID, and the download the Creative Cloud installer.

 

When that finishes, download any of the programs, such as photoshop, by hitting the "try" button.

When the download finishes, no matter what happens, DO NOT HIT OPEN THE APPLICATION. If you do you have to start the entire process over again.

 

 

With your "Universal Adobe Patcher", select photoshop and hit the patch button. Most likely there will be a problem saying that it can't find the file.

Don't sweat.

 

Search for the file by:

 

"This PC" on the left sidebar.

Scroll down and select your "local disk", or you C drive.

Enter the folder called "Program Files" (Not the one with the x86 at the end)

Enter the folder called "Adobe"

Enter the folder called "Photoshop"

Finally, scroll down to the file called "Amtlib.dll", select and then hit "open"

 

The program will automatically patch the file.

 

 

If you want to a desktop icon for the program, just go to your

 

C:\ProgramData\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs

 

and find the shortcut and drag it onto your desktop.
